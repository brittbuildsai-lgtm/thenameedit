Frequently Asked Questions

The 1970s loved names like Jennifer, Amy, Heather, Michelle, and Jessica for girls, and Michael, Christopher, Jason, David, and Matthew for boys. Jennifer was the #1 girls name for the entire decade.

Some are! Names like Heather, Crystal, and Tiffany still feel very '70s-'80s, but others like Michelle, Amber, and Brandon are starting to sound fresh again. Nature-inspired '70s names like Willow and Amber have already returned.

Disco, TV shows like Charlie's Angels, and the continued influence of the counterculture movement all shaped 1970s naming. The decade also saw more creative spellings and unique name choices.
