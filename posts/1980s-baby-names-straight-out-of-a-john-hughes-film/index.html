Frequently Asked Questions

The 1980s were dominated by Jessica, Jennifer, Amanda, Ashley, and Sarah for girls, and Michael, Christopher, Matthew, Joshua, and David for boys. Jessica held the #1 spot for most of the decade.

Some 1980s names like Tiffany, Crystal, and Brandon feel very era-specific, but others like Sarah, Emily, and Alexander are timeless. Many '80s names are just now reaching the age where they'll feel vintage-cool for the next generation.

John Hughes films (Sixteen Candles, The Breakfast Club), soap operas (Dynasty, Dallas), and pop stars (Madonna, Michael Jackson) all heavily influenced 1980s naming trends.
