Frequently Asked Questions

Y2K names are names that peaked in popularity around 1999-2005 — think Britney, Ashley, Tyler, Madison, Cody, and Sierra. They're nostalgic, fun, and many are ready for a comeback.

Some are! Just as 70s and 80s names have cycled back, early 2000s names are starting to feel fresh again. Names like Jordan, Morgan, and Riley never really left, while others like Ashton and Destiny feel retro-cool.

Y2K names tend to be trendy, often celebrity-inspired, and have a certain pop culture sparkle. They include a lot of -den/-ton endings for boys (Jayden, Ashton) and -ey/-y endings for girls (Britney, Ashley, Destiny).
