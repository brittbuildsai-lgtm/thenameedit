1930s Baby Names Straight From Old Hollywood

The 1930s were the golden age of Hollywood glamour — think silver screen sirens, dapper leading men, and names that drip with cinematic elegance. Despite the Great Depression, this decade produced some of the most graceful and enduring names in American history. These names feel like black-and-white movies: timeless, romantic, and impossibly chic.
