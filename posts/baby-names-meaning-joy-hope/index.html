Frequently Asked Questions

Allegra (Italian), Ananda (Sanskrit), Simcha (Hebrew), Makena (Kikuyu), Hani (Arabic), and Nara (Japanese) all express joy or happiness in their native languages.

A name meaning joy is a lifelong positive affirmation. Whether your child looks up their meaning at age 8 or 80, finding 'happiness' waiting there is always a gift.
