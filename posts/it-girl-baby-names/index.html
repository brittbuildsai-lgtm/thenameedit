Frequently Asked Questions

It Girl names have a certain je ne sais quoi — they're stylish, confident, and memorable. They tend to be short and punchy (Gigi, Zara) or elegantly dramatic (Valentina, Cleopatra). The key ingredient is attitude.

Right now, It Girl names include Margot, Sloane, Gigi, Hailey, Zendaya, Suki, Kaia, and Sabrina. They're the names of models, actresses, and trendsetters who define cool.

The boy equivalent would be names with effortless cool — think Remy, Cruz, Nico, Jude, Kai, and Matteo. Same main character energy, different packaging.
