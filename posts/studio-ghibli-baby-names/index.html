Frequently Asked Questions

Beloved Ghibli names include Chihiro (Spirited Away), Kiki (Kiki's Delivery Service), Nausicaä, Ponyo, Satsuki and Mei (My Neighbor Totoro), Howl, Sophie, Ashitaka, and San (Princess Mononoke).

Chihiro is a Japanese name meaning 'a thousand fathoms' or 'a thousand searches.' It's the real name of the protagonist in Spirited Away and symbolizes depth and curiosity.

Many Ghibli names work beautifully across cultures. Sophie, Kiki, Marco, and Fio are easily recognizable worldwide, while names like Mei, Haku, and Sora have a lovely simplicity that travels well.
