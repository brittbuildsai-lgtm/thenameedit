Frequently Asked Questions

The mob wife aesthetic is a maximalist, Italian-American glamour trend — think Carmela Soprano, Karen Hill from Goodfellas, and real-life mob wives. It's all about bold jewelry, fur, leopard print, and unapologetic confidence.

Mob wife names tend to be Italian or Italian-American, bold, and dramatic. They sound like they belong to someone who runs the family — names like Carmela, Valentina, Rocco, and Salvatore.

Many mob wife names are gorgeous mainstream choices! Valentina, Gianna, Marco, and Leonardo are beloved internationally. The aesthetic just adds a layer of glamorous attitude to these beautiful Italian names.
