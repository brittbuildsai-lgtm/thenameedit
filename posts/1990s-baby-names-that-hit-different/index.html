Frequently Asked Questions

The 1990s loved Jessica, Ashley, Emily, Samantha, and Sarah for girls, and Michael, Matthew, Christopher, Jacob, and Joshua for boys. The decade also popularized names like Brittany, Chloe, and Tyler.

Names that peaked in the early '90s are starting to feel vintage-cool again. Look for names like Amber, Megan, and Chelsea to start cycling back, following the roughly 100-year naming cycle trend.

The '90s blended preppy names (Katherine, William) with newer inventions (Destiny, Dakota). It was also the decade where unisex names like Jordan, Taylor, and Morgan really took off.
