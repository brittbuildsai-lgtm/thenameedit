Frequently Asked Questions

Nature names have been used for centuries — think Violet, Rose, and Ivy in the Victorian era. While some newer picks like Juniper are trending, the nature name category has proven staying power.

Many nature names are beautifully unisex or masculine — Forrest, River, Rowan, Hawk, Jasper, Orion, and Brooks all work wonderfully for boys.
