Frequently Asked Questions

Popular names from K-Dramas include Ji-woo, Seo-jun, Ha-na, Min-jun, Eun-bi, and Do-yun. Many K-Drama names carry beautiful meanings related to wisdom, beauty, and virtue.

Cultural appreciation is wonderful, and many Korean names are used internationally. Consider the meaning and pronunciation carefully, and be respectful of the cultural significance behind the name.

Korean names are typically composed of two syllables, each with a specific meaning from Chinese characters (hanja). Common themes include beauty (미/mi), wisdom (지/ji), grace (은/eun), and brightness (빛/bit).
