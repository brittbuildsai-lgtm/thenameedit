Frequently Asked Questions

Many parents find deep satisfaction in a meaningful name — it's a built-in story, conversation starter, and personal blessing. Your child will likely look up their name's meaning at some point, and finding something beautiful there is a genuine gift.

Liora (Hebrew), Lucia (Italian), Hikari (Japanese), and Orion (Greek) all carry light in their meaning. Light-meaning names span nearly every language and culture on earth.

Nadia (Slavic/Arabic), Esperanza (Spanish), and Haven (English) all capture hope and safety. Biblical names like Asher (blessed/happy) and Selah (pause/reflect) carry spiritual optimism.
