Frequently Asked Questions

The 1940s loved names like Mary, Linda, Barbara, Patricia, and Carol for girls, and James, Robert, John, William, and Richard for boys. These names are sturdy, classic, and full of character.

1940s names carry a sense of strength and elegance that reflects the era's spirit. They're practical enough for everyday life but carry a quiet grace — names that feel like they've seen something and come through stronger.

Many 1940s names are cycling back beautifully. Names like Margaret, Eleanor, Ruth, Arthur, and Walter feel fresh and charming to modern ears. The key is choosing ones with timeless appeal.
