Frequently Asked Questions

Names from Bridgerton include Daphne, Eloise, Penelope, Benedict, Colin, Francesca, Hyacinth, and Gregory. The show has also popularized Regency-era names like Prudence, Portia, and Siena.

Regency-era names (roughly 1811-1820 in England) tend to be formal and elegant — think Arabella, Cordelia, Edmund, and Theodore. Many have classical or literary roots.

Most Bridgerton names strike a perfect balance between vintage charm and modern wearability. Names like Eloise, Penelope, and Benedict feel classic without being stuffy.
