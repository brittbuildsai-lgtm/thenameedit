Frequently Asked Questions

Dark cottagecore takes the pastoral, nature-loving aesthetic of cottagecore and adds gothic, witchy elements — think wild herbs, dark forests, folk magic, and Victorian mourning jewelry. The names match this moody, mystical vibe.

Names like Rowan, Hazel, Sage, Raven, Willow, Bramble, Juniper, and Wren all have a natural, witchy quality. Historical witch names like Circe, Morgan, and Sabrina also fit beautifully.
