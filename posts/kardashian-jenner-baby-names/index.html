Frequently Asked Questions

Names like North, Saint, Chicago, Stormi, Dream, True, and Psalm have all been popularized by the Kardashian-Jenner family. Beyond their own children, the family has also brought attention to glam names like Reign, Khloe, and Kylie.

While some names like Stormi and North feel very of-the-moment, many names in their orbit — like Mason, Penelope, and Reign — are classic enough to age well. It all depends on your personal taste.

Kardashian-style names tend to be bold, short, and impactful. They often have a luxury or aspirational quality — think power words, place names, or virtue names with attitude.
