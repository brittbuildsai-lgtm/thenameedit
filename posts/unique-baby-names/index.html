A name is 'too unique' only if it causes your child consistent difficulty — think unpronounceable spellings or offensive associations. The names on this list are rare but recognizable, with clear pronunciations and beautiful meanings.

Today's classrooms are more diverse than ever, and unusual names are increasingly common. Most teachers and kids adapt quickly. A unique name can actually help your child stand out in positive ways.
