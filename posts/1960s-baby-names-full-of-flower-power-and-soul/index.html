The 1960s were a decade of revolution — civil rights, counterculture, flower power, and the Beatles. The names reflect all of it: soulful, spirited, and full of meaning. From the early-sixties elegance of Jackie Kennedy names to the late-sixties bohemian freedom of Woodstock, this decade has range.
