Victorian Baby Names With Timeless Elegance

Victorian names have an elegance that modern names can only dream of. They're formal without being fussy, romantic without being fragile, and they carry a kind of gravity that makes them sound important from the very first introduction. These are names that belong on handwritten letters, engraved lockets, and birth certificates that will age beautifully.
