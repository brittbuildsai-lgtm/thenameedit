Frequently Asked Questions

Names like August, Ivy, James, Dorothea, Betty, Carolina, and Marjorie all appear in Taylor Swift's music. Her folklore and evermore albums are especially rich with vintage, nature-inspired name choices.

August is a beautiful unisex name that's been rising in popularity thanks in part to Taylor Swift's song 'august.' It means 'great' or 'magnificent' and has a warm, late-summer feel.

The folklore era is full of dreamy, cottagecore names like Ivy, Willow, Dorothea, August, James, Betty, and Rebekah. These names feel timeless and literary.
