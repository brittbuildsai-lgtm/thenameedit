About The Name Edit

Choosing a baby name is one of the most personal, exciting, and (let's be honest) stressful decisions you'll ever make. That's why we created The Name Edit — a curated collection of baby name lists organized by aesthetic, vibe, and meaning.

No endless scrolling through alphabetical databases. No overwhelming lists of 10,000 names. Just thoughtfully curated lists grouped by the feeling you're going for — whether that's old money elegance, nature-inspired whimsy, or something short and sweet.

How It Works

Every list on The Name Edit is hand-curated and organized by category. Each name entry includes its meaning, origin, and optional "vibe tags" so you can quickly find names that match your style. Browse by category, search by name, or just explore — you might fall in love with a name you never expected.

Our Philosophy

We believe a name should feel right — not just look good on paper. That's why we organize by vibe rather than just letter or popularity ranking. A name's sound, its cultural weight, and the image it conjures all matter as much as its meaning.

The Name Edit is a labor of love, and we're always adding new lists. If you have a category suggestion or just want to say hi, drop us a line.
